Soda Springs

The annual Geyser Day Celebration will be held at the Soda Springs City Park. The festivities will start with the Beat the Blast 9k fun run at 7:30 AM followed by an opening ceremony at 12:00 PM adjacent to the pavilion at City Park. There will be the fresh wild Alaskan salmon feed, a vendor fair, live entertainment, the Duck Drop and much more.
